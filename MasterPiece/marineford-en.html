MARINEFORD SAGA

Also known as the War Dome Saga, the Marineford War marks the end of the first half of the anime. At this point in the plot, Luffy and the other Straw Hat Pirates are separated in the Sabaody Archipelago. And this is where things seem to become a little more difficult for Luffy and the others.



Saga Arcs:


  • Sabaody Archipelago Arc: episodes 385-407;

  • Amazon Lily Arc: episodes 408-421;

  • Impel Down Arc: episodes 422-425;

  • Marineford Arc: episodes 457-489;

  • Post-Marineford Arc: episodes 490-516.
