ONE PIECE

Board with us on this ship full of adventures and emotions, Master Piece, will introduce you to everything you need to become a real pirate on this journey. One Piece is the longest, longest manga in history, written by Eiichiro Oda. The manga began in 1997, while the renowned anime began in mid-1999. The manga series is licensed and published in Brazil by Panini Comics. The anime series has already been shown in Brazil, and in Portugal. Currently the series is broadcast simultaneously by Crunchyroll, already in Brazil and will be transmitted by the Netflix streaming platform, with new voice acting, don't miss it, sailor!
