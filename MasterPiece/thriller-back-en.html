THRILLER BACK SAGA

In the fifth saga of the anime, Luffy and the crew end up landing on a ghost island called Thriller Bark. On the spot, a strange character uses Luffy's shadow to revive Oars, a legendary giant. With Brook's help, a skeleton, Luffy and the other Straw Hats will have to face Oars and the one who brought him back to life.



Saga Arcs:


  • Thriller Bark Arc: episodes 337-381.
