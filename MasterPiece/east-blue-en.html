EAST BLUE SAGA

We begin with the saga that introduced Luffy to the world, the so-called East Blue Saga. It shows the character as a child living in East Blue and at the beginning of the crew recruitment for him to hunt down the One Piece and try to become the king of pirates.



Saga Arcs:


  • Romance Dawn Arc: episodes 1-4;

  • Orange Town Arc: episodes 5-8;

  • Syrup Village Arc: episodes 9-18;

  • Baratie Arc: episodes 19-30;

  • Arlong Park Arc: episodes 31-44;

  • Buggy's Gang : After the Battle! Arc: episodes 46-47;

  • Loguetown Arc: episodes 45-48-53.
