SAGA YONKOU

We have reached the most current saga of One Piece, entitled Yonkou. An alliance called Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai is formed to take down Kaido. That's when Luffy gets in on the action, gets involved with a Yonko named Big Mom and needs to embark on a rescue mission from his friend, Sanji.
